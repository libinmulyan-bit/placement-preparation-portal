Interview Questions

Tell me about yourself.

Mention your education, skills, projects and career goals.

Why should we hire you?

Discuss your strengths, dedication and willingness to learn.

What are your strengths?

Problem solving, teamwork, communication and adaptability.

What are your weaknesses?

Mention a genuine weakness and explain how you are improving it.

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Talk about career growth, learning and responsibilities.

Why do you want to join our company?

Discuss company reputation, learning opportunities and culture.

How do you handle pressure?

Explain prioritization, planning and staying calm.
